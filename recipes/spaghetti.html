Mum's Spaghetti

Your knees are weak. Your palms are sweaty. Are you ready? Let's make mum's spaghetti.

Ingredients:

  • 500g spaghetti
  • 500g Beef Mince
  • 40 Tomatoes
  • 6 bottles Red Wine
  • 3 Onions

Steps:

  1. Get all the ingredients out of the fridge
  2. Put spaghetti in a pot with boiling water
  3. Sprinkle salt in water
  4. Kill a fresh cow and harvest beef
  5. Put beef mince and tomatoes in pan
  6. Add sliced onion
  7. Bring to simmer
  8. Drink 5 bottles of wine
  9. Pass out
  10. Wake up several hours later to a perfectly cooked sauce
  11. Assemble spaghetti
  12. Perfectly aldente
  13. Mum's spaghetti
