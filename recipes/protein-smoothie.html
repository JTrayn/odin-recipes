Guava Protein Smoothie

  • 500g Guava

  • 900 egg whites
  • 5 tubs Peanut Butter
  • 1 Tbpn Olive Oil

  2. Mix the protein powder with water
  3. Throw every other ingredient in
  4. Drink 900 egg yokes raw
  5. Drizzle some olive oil
  6. Obtain a jackhammer from your local hardware store
